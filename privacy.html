Privacy

Last updated 26 June 2026

This policy describes how slabset.online (“SlabSet”) handles information when you use the calculator.

Calculator inputs

SlabSet is designed to work without an account. Dimensions, prices, and settings you enter are processed in your browser to show results. We do not operate a service that stores your project details on our own servers.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics (Google LLC) to understand how the site is used — for example, pages visited, approximate location, device and browser type, and referral source. Google may process technical data such as IP address. Google’s privacy policy is at policies.google.com/privacy.

You can opt out of Google Analytics in your browser using a Google Analytics opt-out add-on or your browser’s privacy settings.

Hosting

SlabSet is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical connection data required to deliver the site. See GitHub’s privacy statement.

Email

If you contact us by email, we use your email address only to respond.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always on this page.
