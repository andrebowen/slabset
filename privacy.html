Privacy

Last updated 3 July 2026

This policy describes how SlabSet handles information when you use the calculator.

Calculator inputs

SlabSet is designed to work without an account. Dimensions and settings you enter are processed in your browser to show results. We do not operate a service that stores your project details on our own servers.

Hosting

SlabSet may be hosted on static hosting providers (for example GitHub Pages). Those providers may process technical connection data required to deliver the site.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand basic site usage, such as page views, device type and approximate region. Analytics data helps us improve SlabSet. We do not use it to store your calculator measurements or project details on our own servers.

Email

If you contact us by email, we use your email address only to respond.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always on this page.

Contact

Privacy questions: slabset@icloud.com
