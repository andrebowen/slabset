Metric concrete volume calculator for Australia

SlabSet works out how much concrete you need for slabs, strip footings, piers and stairs, and turns it into an order: bags or a ready-mix delivery.

How it's calculated

SlabSet multiplies your measurements and quantity together, then adds your chosen wastage: that's the big number. A 20 kg bag counts as roughly 0.0098 m³ of placed concrete, so about 102 bags make one cubic metre. Ready-mix rounds up to the next 0.1 m³, since that's the step plants sell in.

FAQ

How much concrete do I need?

Pick the shape, enter your measurements and the volume updates instantly, wastage included.

What wastage should I allow?

10% suits a standard site. Drop to 5% for tidy formwork on a compacted base, or go 15 to 20% over rough ground and hand-dug trenches.

Should I order bags or ready-mix?

Under about 0.5 m³ bags usually win on price. Over 1 m³ ready-mix wins on price and convenience, even with a short-load fee.

Are these quantities exact?

They are estimates. Round up on site and confirm quantities and prices with your supplier before ordering.
